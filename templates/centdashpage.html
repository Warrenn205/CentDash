Welcome to CentDash

CentDash is a personal finance application that allows users to keep track of their income, expenses, and spending, and determine their net worth. CentDash offers the following features:

Dashboard

With the interactive Dashboard, you can visualize all your financial data from Budgets and Net Worth. With the graphical representation of your data with cards and bar charts, CentDash provides your financial information with accuracy and ease.

Budgets

Budgets allows you set your budgets by allowing you to provide your income, expenses, and spending. With Budgets, you can add all your streams of income, expenses, and spending and it will calcuate it accordingly based on the user's input of financial data.

Net Worth

Net Worth allows you to determine your net worth. With this feature it is simple to figure out your overall net worth by entering all your assets and liabilities with accurate calcualation.

Calculator

Calculator allows you to calculate your salary in order to determine your earnings and pay.

Statements

With the Statements feature, you can view your financial statements and reports. Statements takes all the financial information from Budgets and Net Worth and display them in reports to let users analyze their spending habits and behavior.
